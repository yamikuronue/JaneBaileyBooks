Short Stories

Jane writes short stories on occasion! Here are some of her select favorites:

  • Boy Meets Book: You know the drill: boy meets book, boy loses book, boy finds book again, happily ever after. A contemporary fantasy story.
  • The Second Ball: After Cinderella leaves for her Happily Ever After, the mice gather to host their own ball. A steampunk romance.
Want more? Jane releases new stories every month on her patreon. For as little as $1/story, you can read them as soon as they're done -- including the ones she hasn't chosen to list here.
